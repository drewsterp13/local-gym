About

Kirks's Small Town Gym is a gym in Reywood, West Virginia located on Ripley Street just off of route 45 in the downtown area, across the local Marron Coffee Shop, this website is to improve the recognition of the gym by locals and travellers alike.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help people from the small town of Reywood, West Virginia to be more healthy, more energetic, and more stronger. We also want to help others to find enjoyment in an active lifestyle. Bodybuilding is certainly not easy and it is not instant gratification like gaming, or using the internet, however, in the long-term, it will be worth it, if you are able to do some physical excersise.

Brief History

A body builder named Dennis Kirk along with his friend Larry Chapman, who are originally from the area, decided to start a small gym in Kirk's parents' garage, later they founded the gym in town in 2015 called: "Kirk & Chapman Local Reywood Gym". While they were great with bodybuilding, they struggled financially, Chapman moved away, and the gym went bankrupt in 2017. However, Kirk did not give up and instead worked at a nearby physical therapy (He went to college earlier). He saved up the money and help from family, friends, and Chapman, Kirk opened the new Kirk's Small Town Gym in 2021, and it has been growing ever since. Kirk really hopes that you can build you body here.
